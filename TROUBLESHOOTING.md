# TROUBLESHOOTING

## Common Issues

### Issue 1: Unable to connect to network
- **Solution:** Check your network settings and ensure that Wi-Fi is enabled.

### Issue 2: Slow performance
- **Solution:** Restart your device and close unnecessary applications.

### Issue 3: Software not updating
- **Solution:** Verify your internet connection and check for updates manually.

## Debugging Tips

1. **Log Files:** Always check log files for error messages.
2. **Reproduce the Issue:** Try to reproduce the issue to gather more information.
3. **Consult the Community:** Utilize forums and community resources for help. 

For any persistent issues, consult the documentation or seek further assistance.
